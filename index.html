About

Hi. I'm Andrew. Also Andy, or Dreey.

I'm a Creative Director and brand strategist based in Nairobi, Kenya. I founded Dreeydesigns in 2019 — not as a freelance side hustle, but as a proper studio with a methodology, a positioning, and a belief that world-class creative work could be built and delivered from right here in Nairobi.

For the past seven years, I've directed campaigns for Range Rover, Land Cruiser Lexus, and Jack Daniel's. I've built brand systems for hospitals, safari companies, churches, retail brands, and tech startups. And I've done it all using tools most agencies in this region haven't even heard of yet.

That gap between what I know and what most are doing? That's the moat. That's Dreeydesigns.

How I got here

The Story So Far

01

It Started With a Sketchbook

Long before brand strategy or client briefs, there was a kid in Nairobi filling notebooks with type experiments, logo sketches, and visual ideas that had nowhere to go yet. I didn't know it then, but I was already doing the work. Art was the language I reached for before I understood it as a career.

02

Trained. Then Self-Taught.

Nairobits Trust. Nairobi Technical Training Institute. Shang Tao Media Art College. I took the foundations seriously. But the real education happened after hours — studying international agencies, reverse-engineering great campaigns, teaching myself what institutions don't have the courage to teach. The industry moves faster than any curriculum. I learned to move with it.

03

Dreeydesigns Was Never a Side Project

In 2019, I launched Dreeydesigns with a clear model: one studio, high-value clients, no compromises on quality. I chose premium positioning deliberately. Within two years, the studio had seven active brand verticals and clients across Kenya, the UK, and the US.

04

When AI Arrived, I Leaned In

When AI tools hit the creative industry, most agencies panicked. I saw an opportunity. I built automation pipelines with N8N, integrated Claude and ChatGPT into my workflow, and started delivering in days what used to take weeks — at higher quality. At Dreeydesigns, AI isn't a replacement for craft. It's the amplifier of it.

05

Nairobi. But Never Small.

I'm proud to build from Nairobi. The city has shaped my eye, my instincts, and my hunger. But I've always designed for a global audience. The brands I build command attention on screens in cities I've never visited. And right now I'm building HADITHI — an African children's animation brand that will bring our continent's stories to the world.

What I Believe

Principles

"For me, design is about commanding attention, communicating with precision, and building something that lasts beyond the brief."

01

AI Is Your Leverage

The creative industry is being reshaped. Most agencies are watching. At Dreeydesigns, we use AI as a competitive moat — delivering brand systems faster, smarter, and at higher quality than legacy studios. This isn't a threat to craft. It is the amplifier of it.

02

Brand Systems, Not Logos

A logo is a signature. A brand system is a language. I build the full vocabulary — visual, verbal, and behavioural — so your brand speaks the same way in every room it enters, long after the project is closed.

03

Story Before Style

Every visual decision is earned by a narrative reason. I start with the story — who you are, what you stand for, who you're talking to — before choosing a font or a colour. Style without story is decoration. Story without style is invisible. Both together? Unbeatable.

04

Nairobi First. World-Class Always.

I am proud to build from Nairobi. Our visual culture, our ambition, our creative instinct is a feature — not a footnote. The world's best creative work will increasingly come from this continent. I am here to make sure it does.
